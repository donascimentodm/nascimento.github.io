About

Dr. Daniele Maria do Nascimento is a researcher who combines molecular diagnostics and bioinformatics to improve plant disease surveillance and reduce agricultural risk. Her work centers on developing, validating, and standardizing diagnostic assays that deliver accurate, actionable pathogen detection for both research and industry applications.

She integrates computational and laboratory approaches—including NGS (next-generation sequencing) data analysis, metagenomics, and targeted assay design—to produce robust, field-relevant diagnostic solutions. Dr. Nascimento also builds resources and workflows that help labs adopt reproducible, validated testing pipelines.

Expertise: molecular diagnostics; assay development and validation; NGS and metagenomic analysis; pathogen identification; diagnostic standardization; translational bioinformatics.

Research Interests

Interests: Plant Pathology, Seed Pathology, Microbial Ecology, Bacteriology, Bioinformatics, Molecular diagnostics, high-throughput sequencing (HTS) technologies, and diagnostic solutions for agriculture.
